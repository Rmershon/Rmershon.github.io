Senior Research

During this semester, Fall of 2017, I am conducting a year long research into the indentification of bots on Twitter. I am using a large data set of over 5.5 million tweets from approx. 36,000 accounts. Using this data I will learn what differentiates a bot from a real account. Through my current readings of previous research on this topic, I have found that none have implemented sentiment analysis into their qualifications. So what I am proposing is that these bot accounts will tend to have a higher average sentiment, either positive or negative, when compared to real users sentiment. This is because real users may go through different emotions. In order to do this I will need to implement some natural language processing and use machine learning algorithms to help quantify the bot accounts for future identification. As I write my paper it will be updated and linked on here.
